# Organizational Evidence: Internal Data Collection Strategy

## Internal Evidence Goals
**Primary Goal:** Collect internal data on employee engagement, turnover, morale, and trust to confirm the extent of cultural integration challenges during M&A.
**Secondary Goal:** Gather internal data on resource availability, leadership alignment, and HR program effectiveness to assess feasibility of proposed cultural integration solutions.

## Available Data Sources

### Performance Metrics/KPIs
- **Financial Data:** Revenue growth, integration costs, profitability margins.
- **Operational Data:** Productivity rates, project delivery timelines, error/quality metrics.
- **Human Resources Data:** Voluntary turnover, absenteeism, employee satisfaction survey scores, performance review ratings.
- **Customer Data:** Customer retention rates, Net Promoter Score (NPS), acquisition churn.
- **Other Relevant Metrics:** Post‑integration synergy realization (cost savings, cross‑selling rates).

### Internal Systems
- **CRM Systems:** Customer churn, retention, and satisfaction data.
- **ERP Systems:** Operational efficiency, supply chain integration metrics.
- **HR Information Systems:** Employee demographics, turnover, engagement survey results, performance ratings.
- **Financial Systems:** Accounting data, integration costs, profitability.
- **Operations Systems:** Service delivery times, production efficiency, error rates.

### Reports and Documentation
- **Regular Reports:** Monthly HR dashboards, quarterly financial reports, annual performance reviews.
- **Survey Results:** Employee engagement surveys, customer satisfaction surveys, stakeholder feedback.
- **Audit Results:** Internal audits of HR processes, external compliance audits.
- **Assessment Reports:** Leadership alignment assessments, cultural due diligence reports.
- **Historical Studies:** Past M&A integration reports, lessons learned documentation.

## Data Collection Plan

### Quantitative Data Collection
#### Metrics to Collect

**Problem-Related Metrics:**
- Metric 1: Voluntary turnover rate – Current value: 18% – Data source: HRIS.
- Metric 2: Employee engagement score – Current value: 62% – Data source: annual employee survey.
- Metric 3: Productivity index (output per FTE) – Current value: 0.85 vs. baseline 1.0 – Data source: ERP/operations reports.

**Solution-Related Metrics:**
- Metric 1: Retention of critical talent – Baseline: 80% – Data source: HRIS talent tracking.
- Metric 2: Leadership alignment score (survey) – Baseline: 65% – Data source: leadership assessment reports.
- Metric 3: Communication effectiveness (survey response clarity) – Baseline: 60% – Data source: pulse surveys.

#### Time Period Analysis
- **Historical Data Range:** Past 3–5 years of HR and financial data.
- **Trend Analysis Period:** Quarterly trends during integration phases.
- **Seasonal Considerations:** Account for annual turnover spikes (e.g., post‑bonus cycles).
- **Baseline Period:** Pre‑M&A year as “normal” operations baseline.

### Qualitative Data Collection
#### Internal Documents Review
- **Policy Documents:** HR integration policies, onboarding procedures, retention program guidelines.
- **Meeting Minutes:** Leadership integration meetings, HR steering committee notes.
- **Communication Records:** Internal memos, CEO announcements, HR newsletters.
- **Project Reports:** Previous integration project outcomes, lessons learned.

#### Observational Data
- **Process Observations:** Onboarding workflows, leadership communication cadence.
- **Workflow Analysis:** How integration tasks are executed across teams.
- **Communication Patterns:** Frequency and clarity of updates across levels.
- **Cultural Observations:** Employee sentiment, informal resistance behaviors, morale indicators.

### Simplified Analysis
Turnover, engagement, productivity trends.
